## Tips for Cold Emailing

1. **Personalize everything**
   - Reference their specific business
   - Mention where you found them (BTC Maps + location)
   - If you've visited their shop, say so!

2. **Keep it short**
   - They're busy. Get to the point.
   - 3-4 paragraphs max.

3. **Lead with value**
   - What's in it for them? (Zero fees, free setup, ownership)

4. **Low-friction CTA**
   - "Would you be open to a quick chat?"
   - "Should I send you the setup guide?"
   - Don't ask for a meeting immediately.

5. **Follow up**
   - 1 follow-up after 3-5 days
   - 2nd follow-up after 1 week
   - After that, let it go.

6. **Track responses**
   - Use a simple spreadsheet
   - Note what works/doesn't

7. **Be helpful**
   - Even if they say no, offer value
   - "No worries — here's a Lightning setup guide anyway"
